## Google Play Store Compliance

The privacy policy URL is required for Google Play Store Data Safety form submission.

All privacy policies include:
- 9 required sections covering data collection, usage, storage, sharing, retention, rights, children's privacy, changes, and contact information
- 8-point legal framework including medical device disclaimers, emergency services disclaimers, no guarantees, user responsibility, limitation of liability, informed consent, and terms acceptance
- WCAG 2.1 AA+ accessibility compliance
- Mobile-responsive design
- Background location usage explanation
